Our Story

A Tradition of Authentic Italian Cuisine

Since 1999, Paparizza has been bringing the authentic taste of Italy to Greater Kailash, Delhi. Our passion for traditional Italian cooking is matched only by our commitment to using the finest ingredients.

Every dish is crafted with love, from our wood-fired pizzas with hand-tossed dough to our pasta made fresh daily. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, ensuring each bite transports you straight to the heart of Italy.

Led by Chef Giuseppe Rossi, who trained in Naples and Rome, our kitchen combines time-honored techniques with contemporary flair.
